About Punarnava Resort & Spa

The Punarnava Resort & Spa is nestled in the scintillating sublime region of Uttarakhand Himalayas in Northern India and exhibits harmonic coexistence with nature. Renowned as ‘Devbhumi’, Uttarakhand (A land of the Gods), this region is blessed with natural beauty, divinity, and spiritual vibrations. We welcome you at this unique luxurious wellness destination aesthetically parked in the dense forest of Mussoorie with a spectacular & stunning George Everest Mountain View. Imbibe the holistic Lifestyle experience in this awe-inspiring existential nature of mystical divinity. Rejoice in the transformation of inner rejuvenation and inner awakening.

Experience the Calmness in the Middle of Green Jungle

During summers, enjoy the forest whispering with the cool breeze caressing your body. See the splendor of the new leaves on trees and the fresh beauty of nature at its full bloom. During rains delight in the chirping of rare birds, relish the juicy seasonal fruits in the alluring landscape of a natural waterfall. During winters bask in the warmth of sunlight surrounded by life -saving herbs, savoring crisp, hot & bio-dynamic food served around a bonfire. During spring enjoy the blooming of natural wildflowers celebrating the BasantMahotsav in its full glory.

Explore Mussoori

5 km

Kempty Falls

A mesmerizing waterfall located 15 km from Mussoorie, Kempty Falls is perfect for picnics, swimming, and relaxation amidst the natural beauty. The cascading waters and serene surroundings make it a popular spot for tourists and nature lovers.

3 km

Landour

A quaint, serene town near Mussoorie, Landour is known for its colonial charm, scenic views, and peaceful walks. It offers a glimpse into the region's history and culture, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and tranquility.

4 km

Dalai Hills

Located near Happy Valley, Dalai Hills features a serene Buddha statue and prayer flags. It offers panoramic views and a tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for meditation, relaxation, and experiencing Tibetan spiritual culture

16 km

Nag Tibba Trek

Nag Tibba is the highest peak in the lower Himalayas, offering a thrilling trekking experience. The trek provides stunning views, diverse flora and fauna, and opportunities for camping, making it a favorite among adventure enthusiasts.

6 km

Cloud's End

Cloud's End marks Mussoorie's geographic end, surrounded by dense forests. This peaceful retreat offers spectacular views, serene walks, and a perfect escape from the town's hustle and bustle, ideal for nature lovers seeking solitude.

12 km

White Water Rafting

Mussoorie offers thrilling white water rafting in icy Himalayan rivers. This adventure sport provides an adrenaline rush as you navigate challenging rapids, enjoying breathtaking natural scenery and an unforgettable outdoor experience for adventure seekers
