The Punarnava
Resort & spa

Nestled in the serene hills of Mussoorie, Punarnava Resort & Spa offers a luxurious retreat with stunning Himalayan views. Our beautifully appointed rooms provide modern comfort and rustic charm. Relax in our state-of-the-art spa or enjoy a refreshing swim with mountain vistas. Savor delectable, fresh vegetarian cuisine at our pure veg restaurant. Explore our spacious lawn for leisurely strolls and outdoor activities, or experience live entertainment in our picturesque amphitheater. Whether seeking adventure or tranquility, Punarnava Resort & Spa is your perfect destination for an unforgettable getaway.
