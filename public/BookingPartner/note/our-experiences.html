Alcor Spa Resorts

Alcor Spa Resorts is a Destination Spa category Resort for Couples and Families located in the 2 km long valley behind the famous landmark Kumbhalgarh Fort. Surrounded by beautiful mountains, this resort is on Majera road that runs behind the Fort at an aerial distance of 1.7 km.

This Resort has more than 10000 plants boosting High Oxygen Levels and an Organic Farm offering you Authentic Organic Food in the restaurant. Also, we have the largest swimming pool & spa lounge in Kumbhalgarh offering authentic spa treatments
